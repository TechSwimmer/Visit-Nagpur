Visit Nagpur

Nagpur, the vibrant "Orange City" of India, delights visitors with its rich cultural heritage, mouthwatering cuisine, and warm hospitality. From historical landmarks to serene natural surroundings, Nagpur offers a diverse blend of experiences that captivate and leave a lasting impression on every traveler.

Top three activities to do in Nagpur

DEEKSHABHUMI

AMBAZARI-LAKE

Ambazari Lake, nestled in Nagpur, is a picturesque and serene water body that offers a delightful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Spanning over 15 acres, the lake is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a popular spot for picnics, leisurely walks, and boating activities.

SWAMINARAYAN-TEMPLE

The Swaminarayan Temple is a majestic architectural marvel, adorned with intricate carvings and vibrant colors, offering a spiritual sanctuary for devotees. With its tranquil atmosphere and devotional ambiance, the temple invites visitors to experience a sense of peace and reverence.
